A Cold Wind Blows...

Deep in the tangled heart of the Blackpine Forest, you tread cautiously beneath a moonlit sky. The trees whisper secrets older than time, and the air grows colder with every step. Your hand rests on the hilt of your blade, senses sharp. Suddenly, the shadows ahead stir... From the darkness emerges a figure; tall, pale, and cloaked in crimson. His eyes glow with unnatural hunger. You've heard tales of him whispered in trembling voices around tavern fires: The Vampire of Blackpine.
This is no myth.
This is your fight.
